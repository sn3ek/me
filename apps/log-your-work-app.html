Log your Work

Log your Work is a light weight app to track your progress.

Just start a timer on your finger tips.

Get notifications when your timer runs in the background after a defined time interval.

Start and stop your timer directly on your wrist with your Apple Watch.

Privacy policy

No data is captured or recorded or sent when using the Log your Work app. In the future the app will offer extensions which can be purchased as In-App-Purchase. Which is not required when using the app and just extends the app by definition.
